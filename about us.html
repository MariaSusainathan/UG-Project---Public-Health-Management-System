POLIO CAMPS


  Polio camps are conducted once in a year by the public health Department to ensure India becomes a   polio free nation. Almost 10000 children get vaccinated in the polio camps conducted all around   India.


HEALTH CAMPS


  Health camps are conducted once in a month by the public health Department to ensure people get   free treatment,medication,medical advices and checkups.


AWARENESS CAMPS


  Awareness camps are conducted once in a month by the public health Department to educate people   about prevention from diseases,educate people about the importance of clean environment and   sanitation and various free checkups are provided.


DENGUE PREVENTION AND CONTROL


   Anti-dengue actions are done by the public health department all over the year to ensure that    people are safe from dengue.Free medication is given to people who have affected by    dengue.Mosquito nets are provided to people to be safe from dengue,During rainy season blankets    are provided.



MALARIA PREVENTION AND CONTROL


  Public health department ensures killing malaria and dengue mosquitos by spraying pesticides all   over tamilnadu.Tips and Medication are provided mainly during the rainy season.People are also   informed how to be safe from malaria and dengue.
